## Overview

<a href="#">One Switch</a> places essential Mac controls directly in your menu bar where they belong. Instead of navigating through System Settings to toggle Dark Mode, enable Do Not Disturb, or adjust display settings, access these controls with single clicks from one convenient location. The time saved across daily use adds up significantly.

The <a href="#">toggle collection</a> covers the settings users actually change frequently. Dark Mode switches appearance instantly. Do Not Disturb silences notifications during focus time. Screen Saver activates on demand for privacy or presentations. Keep Awake prevents sleep during downloads or presentations. These toggles address real daily needs.

<a href="#">Hidden files toggle</a> reveals or hides invisible system files in Finder. Developers and power users frequently need to access dotfiles and system folders. Rather than remembering Terminal commands or keyboard shortcuts, toggle visibility with a click. The toggle state persists until you change it.

The <a href="#">AirDrop discoverability</a> control manages who can send you files. Open to everyone when expecting transfers from strangers at events. Restrict to contacts only during normal use. Turn off completely when you don't want interruptions. This control provides security without complexity.

<a href="#">Display settings</a> include commonly adjusted monitor options. Switch resolution quickly for different tasks. Toggle True Tone on displays that support it. Adjust Night Shift for evening use. These controls save trips to Display preferences.

The <a href="#">Bluetooth and WiFi toggles</a> disconnect quickly when needed. Turn off radios for airplane compliance or security. Reconnect with another click when ready. Faster than Control Center for users who prefer menu bar access.

<a href="#">Mute microphone</a> ensures privacy during video calls and recordings. Toggle input muting system-wide with visibility in the menu bar. Know whether your mic is hot at a glance. Essential for remote work and sensitive environments.
